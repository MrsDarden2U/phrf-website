Our mission

Why we do this

Empowering youth and stabilizing communities through direct action.

We provide emergency aid to those in need while fostering the growth of teens ages 13 to 17 through professional job training, academic tutoring, and life-changing mentorship. The Potter's House Relief Fund is a pillar of support for inner-city and minority communities across three states.

Cincinnati · Dayton · Columbus Covington · Lexington · Louisville Atlanta · Macon · Savannah

Our focus areas

  • Natural disaster relief for families in need
  • Eviction prevention and housing assistance
  • Food assistance programs
  • Career and workforce readiness for teens
  • Mentoring
  • Academic Tutoring and College Grant Support
